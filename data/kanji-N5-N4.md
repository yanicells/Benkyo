# JLPT N5 & N4 Kanji Deck

**Source:** [jlptsensei.com](https://jlptsensei.com/jlpt-n5-kanji-list/) (primary, ordered by frequency of use) + [kanshudo.com](https://www.kanshudo.com/collections/jlpt_kanji) (cross-check; added 的, 可, 身 which were missing from jlptsensei)
**Scope:** 80 N5 + 170 N4 = 250 single-character kanji
**Card type:** All cards are **single-character kanji** (no compound `熟語` cards in this batch).
**Suggested Anki structure:** One top-level deck `JLPT Kanji`, two sub-decks:

- `JLPT Kanji::N5` (80 cards)
- `JLPT Kanji::N4` (170 cards)

Order preserved from source (frequency of use, most common first). Examples are high-frequency words using the kanji.

---
